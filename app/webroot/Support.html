Help

1 Registration

Registration accepts new users, and requires personal details and a subscription payment. The aim of the subscription is to limit users to wholesale dealers only.

A unique CarZapp number is assigned to each new user.

A username is selected by the new user and a password is created

Information collected includes:

Personal Information :

- Name, email, Mobile phone,

Company Information :

- Company name,

- Dealer License Number

Software license numbers that we may interact with:

- Dealers Solutions

- Subscription Plans

Note: The CarZapp company has the right to block a user from registering or using the app.

2 Login

The login works by accepting a Username and password. If a user forget their password, there is a “Forgot password” feature that allows the user to reset theor password based on their email address.

The “keep me logged in” option, keeps the user logged in to the app until they select to log out. Without selecting this option, the app automatically logs out after a prolonged period of inactivity.

3 Home

Welcome and dashboard

Screen contains 2 sections: 1) The flicka image banner; and 2) the dashboard with shortcuts. The flicka banner shows images of cars for sale, arranged in an order that is set by the user. The order is random by default, but can be set according to a choice of: Newest cars first; price (Ascending/Descending); Age (Ascending/Descending). The images are scrolled across by flicking them.

The dashboard includes 9 buttons with summary data:

Cars For Sale (Total cars in database); Cars Followed (Total cars followed); Set&Forget(Total cars in SetnForget); Zapp Chat(total chats unread); my Network (Total dealers in network); My Stock (Total cars in own stock); Flicka ; My Customer (Total Customers); My Transactions (Total incomplete transactions).

4 Flicka

Flicka is a section of the site that display cars quickly by only showing a large image and some summary details. The screen offers 2 methods of scrolling: 1) by flicking through the images; or by clicking on the large Left and Right buttons. The middle star allows you to quickly tag cars you like, and add them to your favourite list.

Flicka allows you to quickly scroll through all cars in the database.

5 Car for Sale

Enter a keyword or search using the drop down filters to find the car you want. Options are to enter a keyword and search all makes, or select filters starting with Make, and then, adding Model, Series, Variant, year and other filters.

6 Set & Forget

This section allows you to setup parameters for car that is required but is not in the complete CarZapp inventory - across all dealers. As soon as a car comes into the collective inventory, a push notification will alert the dealer who has created the SnF record.

Each SnF record may also be tagged against a customers name, if the SnF is for a particular customer,

Each Snf May also be broadcast to a list of Dealers, making them aware that tis car is required.

This feature may be restricted in future and additional charges may apply. (It can consume much server processing power, and incur other data charges)

7 Followed cars

Make a list of favourites, once followed, these cars appear in the favourite list and notifications will be sent if any changes are made to these cars.

8 Zapp Chat

Zapp chat is a real-time n in-app, chat system. Each chat is linked to a car linked to a car, and enables 2 dealers to engage in a chat communication about a particular car. The Zappchats are initiated from the Car listing. After a chat is initiated, it is then visible in the Zappchat module. Users may continue each chat at any stage from the ZappChat module and chat in real time .

9 My Stock

This section displays the cars that belong to each dealer. Cars are added via direct XML datafeeds which come into the app at scheduled times direct from third party data providers. Cars may also be added via manual keyboard entry and upload images from photo’s via the mobile handset image library/camera. The module includes a VIN lookup, which speeds up the process of adding new cars. VIN lookups connect to third party data providers and retrieve all details on a car based on the unique VIN chassis number.

10 My Customer

This module maintains a list of customers. As customers call in to the dealer, the dealer may record the customers name and number and request . The My Customer module provides for a real time customer Relationship management function, with direct phone, SMS and email links to customers, keeping them informed as to car orders/ and request status.

11 My Network

Maintain a personalised list of dealers in each dealers own network. Dealers within your network are available to trade with and communicate via chat. The purposes of My Network is:

§ SetnForget records are only sent within the dealers in “My Network” (Network).

§ View others SetnForget records.

§ Buy and Sell Transactions may only be done within the Network

§ Zappchat is for network members only

§ Emails can only be sent within the Network

§ Dealers can block other dealers from seeing their products or communicating with them if they not in their Network.

12 Invite Dealer

This is a short cut to invite dealers to download the app and join the dealers network. With this link, dealers can quickly invite colleagues to the platform to start trading.

13 My Transaction

This provides a mechanism to enable dealers to Buy and Sell cars in real time. Each car has a BUY button, which sends a message to the owner, indicating a request has come in to purchase that car. To complete the trade, the seller needs to click that they agree to the sale. Once, a car that has been bought, that car will immediately be available for selling to other dealers, in the name of the new owner.

The transaction is not paid for on the app, as there is no financial transaction that takes place on the app. All financial transactions are done outside of the app.

14 Valuations

This feature is not complete yet.

15 Setting

In this section, the following setup parameters are maintained:

§ My Profile of personal information,

§ General - email templates,

§ Notification – Push notification setting preferences,

§ Other – not used yet,

§ Flicka – set the order of cars appearing in Flicka

§ Transfer – Transfer of cars between users, as cars are assigned to employees in a multi user car dealership. By assigning cars, the app transfers them from a default user to another user.

1. About Us

Introduction

CarZapp exists because a Car Dealer saw the opportunity for a contemporary approach to wholesaling across the automotive industry. Over the past two years, the app has been developed by a close knit group of professionals sharing more than 50 years of automotive and IT industry experience.

50 Years of Shared Industry Experience, from 3 three founders, has guided a team of developers and designers to produce this App. It is designed with multiple options to input data, as well as many options to communicate openly between users, in a hassle free way. Carzapp runs on Apple, Android and Windows mobile phones and desktop computers, running Windows OS.

CarZapp has been developed primarily by Australian Car Dealers for Australian Car Dealers in the interest of mutually effective way to buy and sell vehicles. The idea is universal , and is applicable in all countries. It will be made available in other countries over the next year.

Being in the industry ourselves, we understand the continuous and ever-increasing difficulty of sourcing quality stock to meet consumer’s demands.

Philosophy

The CarZapp philosophy is to keep the wholesale car business alive and well .

Mission

The CarZapp mission is to empower dealers to grow their network and business with an easy to use digital platform.

Quick Features

Carzapp enables Dealers to share inventory and connect with each other, within a Dealer exclusive platform.

A full-featured platform with easy communication points so whether you are buying or selling, Carzapp is a must have.

Manage Customer requests and share these with fellow dealers, to accelerate deals.

Move cars between dealers fast, as buy and sell opportunities appear across the network in real time.
